About 

Navigation Assistance and Virtual Intelligence for Student (NAVIST) is a digital solution that aids students navigate through campus with ease. In line with goal 4 of the United Nations 17 sustainable development goal, which is quality education, Navist aims to eliminate the chances of a student missing an important event in  campus such as lectures, tests, or any special activity that may have an impact on the student's academic performance. 

Our goal is to see that we create a platform where students can get accurate directions to anywhere in campus, get updates on events happening in campus, and tailored reminders about academic activities such as lectures, tests, change of venues or any other relevant activity.

We believe that by so doing, we can be able to eliminate the chance of a student missing any important event which may tamper with his/her academic performance.
